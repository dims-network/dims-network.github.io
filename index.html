Dynamic Interaction & Multimodal Signals

The DIMS-network

An open family of tools for visualizing multimodal social-interaction data — time series, video, transcripts, and analyses like RQA, cross-wavelet, and ELAN annotations — to bridge qualitative and quantitative research. One canonical dashboard, its forks for specific studies, and companion Python viewers.

About

The DIMS Dashboard is introduced in: Miao, G. Q., Trujillo, J., Bulls, L. S., Thornton, M. A., Dale, R., & Pouw, W. (2025). DIMS Dashboard for Exploring Dynamic Interactions and Multimodal Signals. Proceedings of the 47th Annual Meeting of the Cognitive Science Society (CogSci 2025). It integrates audiovisual, kinematic, neural, and transcript data in one space so qualitative and quantitative analyses of social interaction can sit side by side.
